What is a ATA Carnet?

It is a passport for goods (a set of colour-coded forms) In

  • Allows duty/tax free admission of goods
  • Goods must be totally re-exported/re-imported within time limits stipulated by Customs authorities

An internationally accepted Customs Document, issued by an authorized Chamber of Commerce.

Allows the temporary import and export of goods without the need for raising bonds, duty, tax and Customs formalities in one or a number of participating countries.

Qatar, ATA Carnets are issued by the Qatar Chamber of Commerce & Industry.

What Items are covered by the ATA Carnet?
  1. Commercial samples and advertising film
  2. Goods for international exhibitions
  3. Professional equipment such as:
  • Press and broadcasting equipment
  • Cinematographic/musical equipment
  • Surgical equipment
  • Educational Equipment
  • Costumes, scenery & other stage properties
Who may use an ATA Carnet?
  • Travelling business/sales executives
  • Technicians
  • Fair exhibitors
  • Professional individuals and teams:
  • Film crew
  • Surgeons
  • Architects
  • Artists
  • Engineers
  • Educationalists
  • Entertainers, etc
